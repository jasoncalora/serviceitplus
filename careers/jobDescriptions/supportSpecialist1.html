The Level 1 Support Specialist will act as part of the Operations Department. The main objective is to increase Operations’ productivity by ensuring efficient and prompt response to customers’ inquiries and product-related issues.

Essential Duties & Responsibilities: Service Desk
  • Ensure satisfaction at all times by providing excellent and timely response to Customer inquiries, issues, and requests
    • Act as a part of contact/first line assistance internal and external customers.
    • Follow through customer feedback.
    • Conduct touch point meetings
    • Help in documentation such as How-To documents
  • Receives and logs all product-related issues raised by customers via phone, email or walk-in to our Service Desk System
    • Supervises, monitors progress from start and end of issue ticket.
  • Coordinate any issues to the Team that needs escalation and further investigation
    • Ensured timely delivery and resolution
    • Assist consultant in gathering necessary details (diagnostic, logs etc) to progress ticket or relevant to resolution.
    • Prepares document for scope of testing, timeline and other activity
    • Work closely with team for prompt and accurate issue resolution.
  • Communicate bug enhancement requests and other critical issues to principal vendors for escalation.
  • Update customers with any resolution and current status on their issues, inquiries and requests
    • Conduct remote session if needed to obtain relevant information for incident isolation and resolution
    • Assist consultant in gathering necessary details (diagnostic, logs etc) to progress ticket or relevant to resolution.
    • Drives technical process and controls adequacy of issue related tickets.
    • FCR (first call resolutions) and how to documents.
    • Troubleshooting, analysis, replication, testing and modifications of issues.
  • Suggests continuous improvements on the Service Desk System
  • Report to the Team any bugs and issues encountered with the Internal System
    • Monitors release notes and tech updates from principals and review if can be release to customers.
  • Establish strong relationship with new and existing customers through positive, responsive and excellent communication
  • Send weekly Status Reports to existing customers with pending issues and requests
    • Documentation of ticket status updates or other requests of customers.
Secondary Responsibilities: Project Support
  • Ensure that licenses and other logistics required for the project are available when needed.
  • Assist Lead consultant during training delivery; logistics, attendance sheets, certificates etc.

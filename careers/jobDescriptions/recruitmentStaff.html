The Recruitment Staff main objective is to increase productivity on specific facet of Human Resources. He/she is responsible in overall recruitment processes, from talent sourcing and attracting candidates to interviewing and hiring great employees. He / she will collaborate with department managers on a regular basis and identify future hiring needs. Ultimately, he or she is opt to ensure the company attracts, hires and retains the best employees, while growing strong talent pipeline.

Essential Duties & Responsibilities:
Sourcing and Recruitment
  • Design and implement overall recruiting strategy
  • Develop and update job descriptions and job specifications
  • Prepare and post jobs to appropriate online job posting site/social media/job boards/newspaper/colleges etc.
  • Promotes employee referral system
  • Source and attract candidates
  • Maintain database of applicants for tracking references
  • Screen candidates resumes and job applications
  • Conduct interviews and administer examination using various reliable personnel selection tools/methods to filter candidates
  • Assess applicants’ relevant knowledge, skills, soft skills, experience and aptitudes
  • Manage employee on-boarding and leaver process
  • Data entry into various HR related system
  • Manage and maintain confidentiality of 201 files
  • Monitor and apply HR recruiting best practices
  • Act as a point of contact and build influential candidate relationships during the selection process
  • Promote company’s reputation as “best place to work”

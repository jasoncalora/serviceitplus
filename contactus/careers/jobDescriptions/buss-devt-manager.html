Assist the company acquire new customers and sell additional products or services to existing customers.A Business Development Manager (BDM) is a high-level sales professional with solid background in Information Technology and Sales.

Essential Duties & Responsibilities:
  • Identifying new sales leads by researching organizations and their needs
  • Proactively communicate on a regular basis with all customers to identify new sales opportunities, provide customer service and ensure account renewals
  • Providing superb customer service and support
  • Respond to Request for Proposals (RFP), in timely and accurate fashion
  • Conduct professional and informative sales presentations
  • Acts as a solutions “evangelist” to help prospective and existing clients understand the value of the products and services that Service IT+ offers
  • Initiates contacts to prospective customers by phone or email.
  • Selling products and services to new accounts
  • Upselling/cross-selling to existing accounts
  • Participates in campaigns for leads generation
  • Confidently and successfully negotiates and closes deals
  • Achieves monthly/quarterly/annual sales goals and objectives
  • Work with customers to identify challenges that our products can solve
  • Build account plans, provide sales pipeline and weekly sales forecasting reports
  • Planning and overseeing new marketing initiatives
  • Provide weekly/monthly opportunity pipeline and report on specific campaigns
  • Participate in coaching sessions to find opportunities for improvement
  • Maintain excellent product knowledge and attend or enroll to all training sessions (on-site or online)
  • Ensure accurate and complete information is captured in CRM system. Clean data when appropriate.

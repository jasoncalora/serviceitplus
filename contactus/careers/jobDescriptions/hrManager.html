Responsible for overall management of the Administrative and Human Resources Department of the company. The HR Manager will oversee, mentor and coach HR Executives to help create expertise in respective fields and deliver excellent HR services to the business including managing day-to-day HR Operations. The HR Manager maintains and enhances the organization's human resources by planning, implementing, and evaluating employee relations and human resources policies, programs, and practices.

Essential Duties & Responsibilities:
Administrative Duties
  • Monitoring and coordinating the renewal of all company clearances, permits, licenses, and insurance coverage
  • Monitoring and coordinating with suppliers for supplies and equipment needs of the company
  • Approval of Messenger’s Dispatch Form for every document pick-up/delivery
Human Resource Duties
  • Recruitment and Onboarding
    • Overseeing the selection, hiring and onboarding of job candidates
    • Conducting In-depth interview to job candidates
    • Ensuring every manpower request meets the recruitment timeline
  • Training and Development
    • Identifying training and development needs within an organization through job analysis, appraisal schemes and regular consultation with department managers
    • Designing and expanding training and development programs based on the needs of the organization and the individual
  • Compensation and Benefits
    • Responsible for overseeing government contributions have been properly remitted
    • Monitoring and researching compensation and benefit trends and reviews salary grading for every position
    • Suggesting benefit programs beneficial for employees
  • Employee Relations and Labor Compliance
    • Strategizing, planning and organizing overall Employee Engagement initiatives to support business results (Company Outing/Team Building, Company Anniversary, Christmas Party, Community Service, etc.)
    • Supports facilitation of coaching, counselling and disciplinary case management
    • Monitoring and ensuring company’s compliance on labor regulations
  • Performance Management
    • Ensuring timely appraisal of employees by training managers to coach and discipline employees and leading managers in conducting performance management of their employee
  • Organizational Development
    • Reviewing, identifying gaps, revising and updating of company policies/code of conduct
    • Provides advises to managers and supervisors in understanding and implementing company policies, human resource processes and practices.

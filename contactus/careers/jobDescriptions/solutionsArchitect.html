The Solutions Architect plays a critical client-facing role that interprets and translates client requirements into a solution that can be configured from a standard set of offerings.

Essential Duties & Responsibilities:
  • Serve as technical day-to-day point of contact of a specific product line for the Sales team and customers.
  • To attend meetings with potential Clients to determine technical and business requirements and ensuring that all necessary information is collated liprior to producing a solution
  • Provide technical solutions in a professional manner and to agreed timeframes
  • Create and confidently deliver technical presentations internally and externally
  • Perform technical development for bespoke solutions as part of a design and development frame work
  • Create internal design configuration documentation including network diagrams with technical explanations
  • Prepare and Respond to RFI/RFP specifications
  • Conduct Solution Demonstration and Proof of Concept
  • Document client feature requests and issues, providing feedback to sales and product management
  • Provide accurate and timely management information, to include - activity reports, deals and prospect qualification
  • Communicate regularly both verbally and in writing, with customers, prospective customers, Sales Teams, and your manager
  • Interface with the implementation / project team, articulating customer requirement, to ensure smooth transition from Sale to Delivery
  • Travels periodically to meet with domestic clients.
Management Responsibilities:
  • Manage a team of Solutions Specialists/Consultants for specific solutions aligned with their expertise.
  • Ensure successful implementation of product/solution and completion of projects in agreed timeline.
